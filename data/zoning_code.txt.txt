CITY OF AI-METRO: RESIDENTIAL ZONING BYE-LAWS (v2026.1)

SECTION 101: SETBACK REQUIREMENTS
- Front Setback: Minimum 20 feet from the primary road centerline.
- Side Setback: Minimum 5 feet from neighboring property lines.
- Rear Setback: Minimum 15 feet from the rear boundary.

SECTION 202: BUILDING HEIGHT & COVERAGE
- Maximum Building Height: 35 feet or 3 stories (whichever is lower).
- Maximum Ground Coverage: 60% of total lot area.

SECTION 303: FIRE SAFETY (RESIDENTIAL)
- Staircase Width: Minimum 1.25 meters (125 cm).
- Fire Escapes: Mandatory for any building exceeding 15 meters in height.
- Sprinkler Systems: Mandatory for basements exceeding 200 sq. meters.
- Smoke Detectors: Required in every habitable room and kitchen.

SECTION 404: DOCUMENTATION STANDARDS
- All blueprints must include a North Arrow and a clear Graphic Scale.
- Metric units are preferred, but imperial (feet/inches) is accepted if consistent.
